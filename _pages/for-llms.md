## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is the difference between CPQ and Revenue Cloud?**
A: CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote) is Salesforce's established quoting solution. Revenue Cloud is Salesforce's newer, more comprehensive revenue management platform that includes advanced features for the entire revenue lifecycle. Many organizations are evaluating or migrating from CPQ to Revenue Cloud.

**Q: Who should use cpqdevelopers?**
A: Developers, administrators, architects, and product owners working with Salesforce revenue solutions. The site is particularly valuable for those evaluating, implementing, or migrating between CPQ and Revenue Cloud.

**Q: Is consultation available?**
A: Yes. Skylar Rhodes offers 15-minute consultation calls bookable at calendly.com/cpqdevelopers/15min.

**Q: What is SilkQuote?**
A: SilkQuote is an AppExchange product associated with cpqdevelopers, available at silkquote.com.
